Recipe

Farro Salad

Ready in 45 minutes
Serves 4 people

Tips

You can add any nuts, greens, or vegetables that you like in this. You can also dress the farro with a simpler Italian dressing if you don't like mustard.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup farro
  • 1 large sweet potato
  • 1 cup roughly chopped baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup roasted almonds
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped parsley (opt)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon mustard (whole grain is very good)
  • 1/2 teaspoon maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Soak the farro for 6-8 hours (or overnight).
  2. Cook the soaked farro in salted water.
  3. Chop the sweet potato into quarter inch cubes.
  4. Cook the sweet potato in a skillet with some oil, salt, and pepper until fully tender. (You can also roast in the oven for 20-25 minutes.)
  5. Roast the almonds on medium heat till fragrant. Toss in pan often to prevent burning.
  6. Roughly chop almonds.
  7. Make mustard vinaigrette. Whisk together apple cider vinegar, mustard, olive oil, and maple syrup. Keep this dressing seperate if you are meal prepping.
  8. Assemble the salad. Add spinach, farro, sweet potato, toasted almonds, and feta to a bowl. Mix with dressing as desired.
  9. Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste and give a final mix.
