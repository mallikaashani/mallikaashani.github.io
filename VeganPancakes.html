Vegan Protein Pancakes

Ready in 30 minutes
Serves 1 person

Tips

I love these pancakes with some nut butter and fruit compote, it's like a fancier PB&J. These go great with a simple drizzle of maple syrup too. For a quick fruit compote, heat some frozen berries with a bit of sugar and water. Once bubbling, add a tsp of cornstarch dissolved in water. Let the mixture heat through and let cool. It will thicken as it cools.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oat flour
  • 1 tbsp hemp seeds
  • 1 tbsp ground flax seeds
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tbsp pumpkin seeds
  • 1 banana (the riper the better)
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 pinch of chai masala (ground ginger, cardamom, cloves)
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • coconut oil for cooking

Preparation

  1. Make an egg replacement. Add the chia seeds and ground flax seeds to a bowl with enough water or almond milk to cover. Let this mixture sit until it thickens, 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add a mashed banana to the mixture. Mix in vanilla extract and hemp seeds and pumpkin seeds.
  3. Add your dry ingredients. Add the oat flour, baking powder, cinnamon, chai masala, and salt.
  4. If the batter gets too thick, add some almond milk or water.
  5. Cook your pancakes. Heat a pan on medium heat. Add in a bit of coconut oil.
  6. Use roughly 1/2 cup of batter for each pancake. Cook for 4-5 minutes until golden brown on each side.
  7. Serve warm with toppings of choice.
