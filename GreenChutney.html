Green Chutney

Ready in 20 minutes

Tips

I keep small containers in the fridge and some in the freezer to defrost later. I love a sandwich with some chutney, cream cheese, and a fried egg.

Ingredients

  • 1 inch ginger
  • 2 serrano chillies
  • 2 bunches cilantro
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • juice of half a lime
  • Salt to taste

Preparation

  1. Prepare ingredients. Roughly chop ginger and chillies.
  2. Roast the peanuts in pan until fragrant. Remove skin if necessary.
  3. Roughly chop cilantro (leave stems in).
  4. Blend. Add chopped cilantro to blender with as little water as necessary.
  5. Squeeze in the lime juice, add salt, ginger, and chillies. Blend until no large pieces of ginger.
  6. Add the peanuts and blend until smooth. Leave some texture in the chutney.
  7. Taste for seasoning and adjust.
  8. Separate into containers, to freeze or store in fridge.
