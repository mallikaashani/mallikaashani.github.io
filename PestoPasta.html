This is an easy pesto pasta that comes together in the blender, but feels fancy enough to serve guests.

Recipe

Pesto Pasta

Ready in 25 minutes
Serves 4 people

Tips

Cashews could be used as a richer substitute for walnuts. Make sure you reserve pasta water to adjust your sauce. Be careful with adding salt as the parmesan and pasta water are already salty. You can also add vegetables to this dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 servings spaghetti
  • 1 cup Thai basil
  • 2-3 garlic cloves
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1/2 cup shredded parmesan
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice (opt)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water.
  2. Blend olive oil, walnuts, and peeled garlic cloves.
  3. Add pasta water as necessary to enable blending. Add in Thai basil (and/or other herbs of choice) and parmesan cheese.
  4. Add pasta water as needed to adjust the thickness of the sauce. Taste the sauce and adjust seasoning.
  5. Squeeze in some lemon juice to brighten all the flavors.
  6. Pour the sauce over drained al dente pasta. Mix together.
  7. Serve with some freshly grated Parmesan and some additional black pepper.
