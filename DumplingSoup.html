Miso Dumpling Soup

Ready in 10 minutes
Serves 2 people

Tips

If your dumplings are coated in flour, cook them separately.

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 large bok choy
  • 1 inch ginger sliced
  • 6-8 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 4 inch piece of kombu
  • 2-3 tablespoons white miso
  • 16 frozen dumplings
  • green onion for garnish
  • Soy sauce to taste (opt)

Preparation

  1. Boil 8 cups of water with the kombu, ginger, and dried shiitake mushrooms.
  2. Slice up carrots and bok choy, leaving the leafy parts aside.
  3. Once the mushrooms have softened, remove them and slice them.
  4. Remove the kombu. Add in all the vegetables (mushrooms, carrots, bok choy- except leafy parts).
  5. Let the soup come to a boil and add dumplings.
  6. When dumplings are nearly done, add the leafy parts of the bok choy.
  7. Take the soup off the heat and mix in the miso paste.
  8. Serve hot. Garnish with green onions and add soy sauce to taste.
