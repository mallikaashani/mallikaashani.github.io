Roasted Vegetable Salad

Ready in 15 minutes
Serves 4 people

Tips

For Mexican flavors, use taco seasoning and add corn and black beans when assembling. For Mediterranean flavors, use Italian seasoning and add tomatoes, olives, and feta. The leftover roasted vegetables can also be used as a side dish, in a frittata, oras a filling for a wrap.

Ingredients

  • Cauliflower or broccoli
  • Sweet potato
  • Red onion
  • Chickpeas
  • Baby kale or spinach
  • ¼ cup Tahini
  • 2 tbsp lime juice
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Seasoning of choice: Taco seasoning, salt and pepper, Italian season, etc.

Preparation

  1. Roast your vegetables. Cut cauliflower into bite size florets and sweet potato into ½ inch pieces.
  2. Coat your vegetables in a couple tablespoons of oil and your seasoning of choice. You can also roast your chickpeas.
  3. Roast on a sheet pan for 30 minutes.
  4. Mix your dressing. Add lime juice, grated garlic clove, salt and pepper to tahini. Slowly whisk in water until you get your desired consistency.
  5. Assemble your salad. Pour dressing over greens of choice and mix. Add in slightly cooled vegetables.
  6. Customize with your mix ins: tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, corn, beans, olives, artichoke hearts.
